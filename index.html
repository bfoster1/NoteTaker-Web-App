About

Â Using HTML, CSS, Javascript and Javascript libraries, such as Angular and Bootstrap, NoteTaker was created to provide users with an easy and efficient alternative to produce and store notes. Whether you need to create an outline for a research document or jot-down information from a communication or lecture, NoteTaker is the perfect platform for quick notetaking. For easy scanning and readability, feel free to add color by clicking on the "add color button".

Once the user has completed the note-taking process, he or she has the option to either copy-and-paste the notes into a word document processor or export the notes to a png image - the choice is yours! To get started, begin typing your notes in the respective text fields and the notes will appear on the right in REAL TIME. Don't worry about formatting, NoteTaker has you covered!

* Export button does not work in Safari. A notecard gallery and additional export options coming soon!
